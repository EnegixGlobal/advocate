Meet the Advocate

Advocate Ramesh
Kumar Singh

LLB, LLM — Allahabad University Sonbhadra, Uttar Pradesh

Advocate Ramesh Kumar Singh was born in a small village in Sonbhadra district. Growing up, he watched farmers lose their land to powerful landlords, women suffer in silence due to dowry harassment, and laborers get cheated of their wages — all because they had no legal representation. This injustice drove him to law school.

After completing his LLB and LLM from Allahabad University, he returned not to the big city but to the villages he grew up in. For over 15 years, he has fought in district courts, the High Court, and labor tribunals — always keeping his fees transparent, consultations free, and communication in simple language.

His belief: "The day a farmer knows his rights, the true democracy begins."

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver affordable, accessible, and comprehensible legal assistance to every rural citizen of India — ensuring that poverty and distance are never obstacles to justice. We believe that a fair legal system starts with informed, represented communities.

Our Vision

A rural India where every farmer, laborer, and woman knows their legal rights — and is empowered to exercise them without fear. We envision legal awareness as a catalyst for true democratic equality in every village of this country.

Our Journey

Milestones Over the Years

2009
Law Degree Completed

Graduated with LLB and LLM from Allahabad University. Enrolled with the Bar Council of Uttar Pradesh.

2010
First Case — Land Dispute Victory

Won back a farmer's 3-acre land illegally occupied for over a decade. This victory shaped the rest of his career.

2014
Free Legal Aid Center Opened

Established Sonbhadra's first free legal advice center for farmers, laborers, and underprivileged women.

2018
50-Village Awareness Campaign

Conducted legal awareness camps across 50+ villages covering RTI, women's safety, and labor rights.

2022
Recognized as Best Pro Bono Lawyer

Awarded by the District Court for outstanding contribution to free legal services for rural communities.

2026
Digital Presence & Expansion

Launched online platform to reach even more rural families — making consultation requests accessible from any village.
