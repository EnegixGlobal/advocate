Getting Here

How to Reach Our Office

By Bus

Direct bus services from Varanasi, Mirzapur, and Allahabad to Sonbhadra are available daily. From Robertsganj Bus Stand, our office is a 5-minute walk towards District Court.

By Train

Nearest railway station: Chopan (15 km) and Mughal Sarai (60 km). Auto-rickshaws and taxis are available from both stations to our office.

Can't Travel?

If you cannot travel to our office, we can come to you. Call or WhatsApp to schedule a village visit. We also offer phone consultations at no charge.

Emergency Legal Help — Available 24/7

Wrongful arrest? Immediate domestic violence threat? Urgent court order required? Don't wait until morning. Call us any time of day or night — we respond to genuine emergencies immediately.
