Who We Are

A Village Son,
Fighting for Every Village

Born and raised in rural Uttar Pradesh, Advocate Ramesh Kumar Singh witnessed firsthand how farmers, daily wage workers, and women were denied justice — not because they were wrong, but because they couldn't afford representation.

After graduating from Allahabad University with LLB and LLM degrees, he returned to serve the very communities he grew up in. Today, his practice spans land disputes, women's rights, labor law, and RTI — all offered with free first consultations and services in local languages.
